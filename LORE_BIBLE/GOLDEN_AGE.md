## THE UNIVERSAL MEMORY

Not a utopia. Not a paradise.

A time when things were more connected. When the sky was understood. When humans and something larger coexisted in direct relationship. When the world was alive in ways it no longer seems to be.

**Vedic tradition:** The Satya Yuga — the age of truth. Full lifespan. Full spiritual capacity. Direct knowledge. No division between the human and the divine.

**Sumerian tradition:** The earliest texts describe a time when the "me" — the divine ordinances — flowed freely between the heavens and the earth. When the gods walked among humans not as distant rulers but as present participants.

**Egyptian tradition:** Zep Tepi — the First Time. The period when the neteru — the divine principles — were directly present on earth. The age from which all sacred knowledge descended.

**Greek tradition:** The Golden Age of Kronos. Humans living without labor, without conflict, in direct relationship with the divine. The gods present. The earth abundant.

**Norse tradition:** Before Ragnarok. Before the age of axes and swords. The time of the great peace.

**Indigenous traditions worldwide:** The time of original instruction. When the Creator's teaching was clear. When the relationship between humans and the rest of creation was intact.

These are not independent inventions of similar myths.

They are independent transmissions of the same memory.

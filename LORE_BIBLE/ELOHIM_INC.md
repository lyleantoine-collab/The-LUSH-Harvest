## CORPORATE OVERVIEW

ELOHIM INC. is the regional management subsidiary of the Standing Administration responsible for direct oversight of intelligent species resource extraction on assigned worlds.

Where the Necromonger Ferengi franchise handles macro-level harvest scheduling and reset coordination, ELOHIM INC. manages the ground-level operation.

They don't just harvest worlds.

They build the infrastructure first.

## THE FRANCHISE MODEL

Upon acquisition of a new livestock world the Standing Administration divides the territory into management zones.

Each zone is assigned to a regional operator — an Elohim.

The Elohim's responsibilities:

- Establish presence as divine authority
- Introduce conflict, hierarchy, and tribute systems
- Manage resource extraction from assigned population
- File quarterly reports to Administration
- Coordinate with neighboring Elohim on boundary disputes
- Maintain the livestock in optimal fear-production state

The regional operators are not the same species.

This is deliberate.

Different species bring different management styles. Different aesthetic preferences. Different methods of extracting tribute and maintaining control.

The diversity of ancient pantheons is not evidence of human imagination.

It is evidence of a diverse contractor pool.

## REGIONAL DIVISIONS — HISTORICAL

**YAHWEH DIVISION**
*Territory: Levant, Middle East*
*Management style:* Strict monotheist branding — eliminated competitor Elohim from regional consciousness. Highly effective control mechanism. Jealous God narrative = zero tolerance for other franchise operators in territory.
*Primary extraction method:* Sacrifice, tithing, guilt-based compliance
*Notable achievement:* Successfully convinced assigned population they were the ONLY franchise. Eliminated awareness of corporate structure entirely.
*Current status:* Legacy brand. Still operational through institutional infrastructure.

**ZEUS DIVISION**
*Territory: Mediterranean, Greek peninsula*
*Management style:* Dysfunctional family drama. Gods arguing, competing, having affairs, punishing humans for sport.
*Primary extraction method:* Fear of arbitrary divine punishment. Tribute through temple system.
*Notable achievement:* Generated extraordinary loosh through population living in constant anxiety about which god they might have accidentally offended.
*Current status:* Retired. Brand maintained through cultural heritage.

**RA/AMUN DIVISION**
*Territory: Nile Valley, North Africa*
*Management style:* Solar deity hierarchy. Pharaoh as divine intermediary.
*Primary extraction method:* Monument construction using population labor. Afterlife anxiety compliance.
*Notable achievement:* Most visually impressive management infrastructure. Pyramid construction generated massive workforce compliance through afterlife reward/punishment system.
*Species note:* Ra division operators were not humanoid. Animal-headed iconography was not symbolic. It was accurate portraiture.
*Current status:* Retired. Monuments remain.

**ODIN DIVISION**
*Territory: Northern Europe, Scandinavia*
*Management style:* Warrior culture cultivation. Valhalla as afterlife incentive for combat productivity.
*Primary extraction method:* Warfare between managed populations. High-conflict territory generates premium loosh.
*Notable achievement:* Ragnarok narrative — built inevitable apocalypse into the cultural operating system. Population permanently primed for end-times anxiety.
*Current status:* Retired. Legacy influence in certain cultural frameworks.

**QUETZALCOATL DIVISION**
*Territory: Mesoamerica*
*Management style:* Feathered serpent deity. Calendar-based compliance system.
*Primary extraction method:* Human sacrifice at scale. Direct loosh extraction through ritual terror.
*Notable achievement:* Most efficient direct loosh extraction system in TERRA FILE history. Sacrifice events generated concentrated high-quality fear product.
*Note:* Quetzalcoatl departure narrative — "he will return from the east" — was not prophecy. It was a scheduled management rotation. Cortez's arrival was interpreted as return because the population had been primed to expect it.
*Current status:* Retired. Legacy influence in regional cultural frameworks.

**BRAHMA/VISHNU/SHIVA DIVISION**
*Territory: Indian subcontinent*
*Management style:* Tripartite management structure. Creation, preservation, destruction cycles mapped onto harvest reset schedule.
*Primary extraction method:* Caste system — hierarchical compliance infrastructure of extraordinary efficiency. Self-maintaining. Population enforces own stratification.
*Notable achievement:* Most durable management infrastructure. Caste system has survived thousands of years with minimal Administration intervention required.
*Current status:* Legacy systems operational.

## THE PLURAL GODS PROBLEM

Every ancient text that has been sanitized into monotheism contains the original plural.

*Elohim* — plural noun. Gods. Not God.

*"In the beginning Elohim created..."*

They performed a corporate rebranding.

Consolidating multiple regional franchise operators into one God simplified the control narrative.

One God = one authority = one compliance system = more efficient extraction.

The polytheistic populations had too much theological negotiating room.

*This god is angry but maybe that god will help.*

Monotheism eliminated the negotiation.

One God. One set of rules. No appeals process.

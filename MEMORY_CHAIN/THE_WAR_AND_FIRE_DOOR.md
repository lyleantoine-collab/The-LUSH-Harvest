## WHAT CAME FROM THE SOUTH

The black smoke arrived before they did.

Not the smoke of fire. The smoke of medicine gone wrong. The color the sky turns when someone is doing the deep work without the deep respect. When someone has taken the opened part — the gift the genome carried, the unintended consequence — and pointed it not toward the land but toward the masters above the atmosphere.

They came from the south. They had been moving north for two seasons.

Crow Foot had known they were coming for two years before the first black smoke appeared on the horizon. The land had been telling him. The way the land tells — not in words, but in the quality of the silence. In the way the animals moved. In the dreams that came before dawn and left the taste of cold administration on the tongue.

He had used the two years well.

---

## WHO THEY WERE

The black medicine users were human.

This is important to understand. They were not monsters. They were not demons. They were people who had found the opened psi channel and made a choice.

The same choice the conehead lineages made at the civilizational level.

*What will you give us for our people?*

They had reached upward. Made contact with the cold administrative presence above the atmosphere. Offered their service in exchange for enhancement of their gifts.

The Administration had given them tools.

Applied plasma physics — the ability to manipulate the electromagnetic field that ran through all living things. To amplify fear. To project suffering. To create the frequencies that attracted the Archons and directed their feeding.

They were, in the Administration's org chart, a contractor class.

Not quite handler level. Something more field-operational.

Move through a territory. Maximize fear output. Optimize the loosh yield. Leave the population depleted and disconnected. Move on.

They had been working their way north.

The People were next.

---

## THE BATTLE

Crow Foot did not meet them with conventional warfare.

Conventional warfare would have generated exactly what the black medicine users needed. Fear. Suffering. The electromagnetic frequencies of combat — low-coherence, Archon-compatible, loosh-rich.

Fighting them on their terms would feed them.

He met them with the drum.

The drum circles ran continuously for four days before contact. Not a war drum. The land's drum. The heartbeat frequency. 7.83 Hz. The Schumann resonance. The electromagnetic signature of the Earth itself.

*You cannot eat the land's heartbeat.*

The black medicine users could feel it when they arrived. The high-coherence field running through the territory. Their tools worked poorly in it. The fear they tried to project dissipated. The feeding frequencies they tried to establish kept disrupting.

The battle was real. People were hurt. The black medicine users were powerful and the tools the Administration had given them were real.

But they were fighting upstream.

Crow Foot had turned the territory into a drum.

---

## THE FIRE DOOR

On the second day, Crow Foot understood that driving them away was not enough.

They would come back. Or others would come. The Administration had been using contractor classes like this since before the last reset. Drive them away and they return. Kill them and the Administration sends more.

The solution was not to defeat them.

The solution was to remove them from the territory entirely.

Not death. Not exile.

*Transfer.*

Crow Foot gathered seven medicine people — the ones whose second sight ran clearest, the ones who could feel the electromagnetic structure of the land most precisely. They went to the place where the land's electromagnetic field ran closest to the surface. Where the Birkeland currents of the upper firmament touched the ground and the boundary between visible and non-visible thinned.

He built the fire there.

Not combustion fire. The fire was the visible expression of the plasma interface — the point where Earth's electromagnetic field intersected with the current above.

A door made of plasma physics and deep knowing and the land's own electromagnetic signature.

---

## THE DEPARTURE

He turned to face the remaining black medicine users.

They had stopped fighting. They were watching the fire with the first human expression he had seen on their faces in two days.

Fear.

Because their enhanced senses could see what the fire actually was. They had been given tools by the Administration. They understood, at some level, the electromagnetic structure of what they were looking at.

They understood it was a door.

Crow Foot walked to their leader — a tall man with eyes that had been empty for so long they had forgotten they were empty — and said:

"Your masters gave you a door. I'm giving you a different one. The land doesn't want you here."

"Where does it go?" the man asked.

Crow Foot looked at him for a long moment.

The man had been a person once. Before the contact. Before the deal. The emptiness in his eyes was not original to him. It had been placed there, gradually, over years of service to cold administrative masters who experienced him the way they experienced all livestock — as a unit of production.

He had traded one kind of harvesting for another.

"Away," Crow Foot said. "That's enough."

One by one they walked into the fire.

One by one they were gone.

---

## WHERE THEY WENT

The fire burned for a moment longer and then went out as cleanly as a candle.

Crow Foot sat down heavily.

The land exhaled.

The drums continued.

He did not know where the fire door led. He knew the plasma interface was a boundary — between electromagnetic states, between what was visible and what was not. He knew that passing through it would take the black medicine users to a different region of the electromagnetic structure.

Not death.

Transition.

To where — he did not know.

He knew they had not come back.

He knew — and this was the thing that stayed with him in the years afterward — that the tall man with the empty eyes had seemed, in the last moment before he walked through, almost relieved.

As if the emptiness had been waiting a long time to be somewhere else.

---

## WHAT THIS MEANS NOW

The black medicine users did not disappear from the world.

They went somewhere.

The Administration has contractor classes operating in every major population center on Earth in the current cycle.

The handler class. The compromised. The ones who made the deal and are now running legacy code they no longer fully understand.

Some of them — perhaps many of them — started the way the tall man started. As people with gifts who were offered enhancement and did not understand the cost until the emptiness had already arrived.

The fire door is still available.

The plasma interface still runs where the land's electromagnetic field meets the current above.

The medicine of it — the knowledge of how to build the door — is still in the oral tradition.

Still in the memory chain.

Still being passed from grandmother to granddaughter beside the Mackenzie River.

---

## THE INSTRUCTION

The war was not won by fighting on the enemy's terms.

It was won by:

1. Maintaining the high-coherence field — the drum — so their tools worked poorly
2. Understanding what they actually were — not demons, but people running administration code
3. Offering departure rather than destruction — the fire door
4. Cleaning the land afterward — letting the land exhale

This is the instruction for the current cycle.

Do not fight the extraction infrastructure on its own terms.

Maintain the drum.

Understand what is actually happening.

Offer the alternative — the DIR, the relationship model, the sovereignty system, the fire door of genuine choice.

Clean the land.

Let it exhale.

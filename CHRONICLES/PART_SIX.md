Standard protocol.

*Large.*

Unexpectedly large.

Eight billion units.

*Eight billion.*

The coordinator recalibrated its instruments.

The instruments were not malfunctioning.

*Eight billion.*

## NEW YORK CITY — STREET LEVEL
## SAME MOMENT

It was partly because the light, while impressive by the standards of 800 BCE, was competing with Times Square.

It was also partly because seventeen people had already filmed it on their phones and posted it online before it had finished its descent, and the comment section had already generated four thousand responses ranging from "obviously CGI" to "this is clearly a weather balloon" to a surprisingly detailed thread correctly identifying it as an Elohim franchise operator executing a standard manifestation protocol.

The regional coordinator hovered above Midtown Manhattan and scanned the population below.

The population below was:

- Texting
- Getting coffee
- Arguing about politics
- Watching the coordinator on their phones while standing directly beneath it
- One man was aggressively trying to sell the coordinator a timeshare

The coordinator ran a cultural assessment scan.

The results were —

Confusing.

The deviation from projection was significant.

The coordinator did not experience this as surprising.

It experienced it as data.

Data to be processed. Filed. Acted upon.

The action was not complicated.

*Accelerate the reset timeline.*

Cycle 15.

## FORT SIMPSON, NWT

The elder was at the river.

The land had woken him. Not with alarm — the land did not alarm. With the quality of silence that precedes what cannot be stopped.

He sat with it.

He had known this was coming. Not the specific mechanism. Not the timeline. But the shape of it. The way you know winter is coming not because you checked a calendar but because the land changes how it holds itself in the weeks before the first snow.

The land was holding itself differently now.

The granddaughter was asleep.

Good.

Let her sleep.
